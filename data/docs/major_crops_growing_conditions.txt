Major Crops and Their Growing Conditions

This document summarizes key agricultural crops, their growing seasons, climate requirements, and duration.


Rice
Crop Type: Kharif
Growing Season: June to September (Monsoon)
Climate Requirement: Warm and humid
Temperature Range: 20–35°C
Water Requirement: Requires standing water during most of the growing period


Wheat
Crop Type: Rabi
Growing Season: October to March (Winter)
Climate Requirement: Cool climate during growth
Harvest Condition: Warm and dry weather
Special Note: Sensitive to high temperatures during early growth


Cotton
Crop Duration: 6–8 months
Climate Requirement: Warm climate
Rainfall: Moderate rainfall
Other Requirements: Plenty of sunshine throughout the growing period


Sugarcane
Crop Duration: 12–18 months (Long-duration crop)
Climate Requirement: Hot and humid
Water Requirement: Adequate and continuous water supply
Special Note: Requires fertile soil for high yield


Maize
Crop Type: Grown in both Kharif and Rabi seasons
Climate Requirement: Moderate temperature
Soil Requirement: Well-drained and fertile soil
Special Note: Highly adaptable crop with wide usage
